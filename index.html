About
Me

I am a Masters in Finance student at London Business School, focused on public markets. Before LBS, I spent five years across capital markets and fintech in Latin America — the kind of markets where inflation is double-digit, FX controls are real, and political risk is not a footnote.

At MercadoLibre, the largest technology company in LatAm by market cap, I ran investment product, supporting the migration of a $4.2B in-house fund and the launch of Mercado Pago Asset Management. Before that, I was a founding member of the trading desk at Inviu, a $2B+ AUM investment platform, where I built the infrastructure from scratch.

The part that makes me a bit different: I sit at the intersection of markets knowledge and product execution. I have built trading desks, integrated live market data into client platforms, and written SQL to answer questions that Excel was too slow for. My interest is in businesses that are misunderstood, not just undervalued.

Outside markets, I am usually reading on something I do not understand yet.
