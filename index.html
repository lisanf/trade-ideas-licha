About
Me

I am a Masters in Finance student at London Business School.

Before LBS, I spent more than seven years across capital markets and fintech in Latin America, the kind of markets where inflation is double-digit, FX controls are real, and political risk is not a footnote.

Most of that time was at companies like MercadoLibre (NASDAQ: MELI), the largest technology company in LatAm by market cap, and Inviu, a subsidiary of Grupo Financiero Galicia (NASDAQ: GGAL). I worked in product across both — closer to markets than most product people, closer to tech than most markets people.

I find data genuinely interesting. The kind of person who opens a terminal when something does not add up, and writes the query to check. Not the loudest in the room about it.

Outside markets, I am usually reading on something I do not understand yet.
